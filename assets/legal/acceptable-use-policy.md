# Acceptable Use Policy

**Last Updated:** January 11, 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of the Doppel Talk service. By using our Service, you agree to comply with this policy.

---

## 1. Purpose

Doppel Talk provides voice generation and cloning technology. This powerful technology comes with responsibility. This AUP ensures our platform is used ethically and legally.

---

## 2. Prohibited Content

You may NOT use Doppel Talk to create, distribute, or store:

### 2.1 Impersonation & Deepfakes
- Voice content that impersonates real individuals without their explicit consent
- Deepfakes or synthetic media intended to deceive
- Content designed to mislead others about its synthetic nature
- Fraudulent voice content for scams or identity theft

### 2.2 Illegal Content
- Content that violates any applicable law or regulation
- Content that infringes on intellectual property rights
- Content promoting illegal activities
- Content that violates export control laws

### 2.3 Harmful Content
- Harassment, threats, or intimidation
- Hate speech or discrimination
- Content promoting violence or self-harm
- Sexually explicit content involving minors
- Content that exploits or endangers children

### 2.4 Spam & Abuse
- Unsolicited bulk communications
- Robocalls or automated calling abuse
- Phishing or social engineering attacks
- Malware distribution

---

## 3. Voice Cloning Ethics

### 3.1 Consent Requirements

When cloning a voice, you MUST:
- Have explicit consent from the voice owner
- Be the voice owner yourself
- Use voices you have legal rights to clone (e.g., licensed voice actors)

### 3.2 Documentation

We recommend maintaining:
- Written consent records
- Clear documentation of voice ownership
- Records of licensing agreements

### 3.3 Prohibited Cloning

You may NOT clone:
- Public figures without authorization
- Deceased individuals without estate permission
- Children under 18 without parental consent
- Any voice obtained without proper consent

## 4. API Usage

### 4.1 Fair Use
- Respect rate limits and quotas
- Do not attempt to circumvent usage restrictions
- Do not resell API access without authorization

### 4.2 Security
- Keep API credentials confidential
- Report any security vulnerabilities
- Do not probe or test system security without permission

### 4.3 Integration Requirements
- Clearly disclose AI-generated voice content where required by law
- Do not use API to build competing services without permission
- Maintain audit logs of API usage

## 5. Commercial Use

### 5.1 Permitted
- Creating voice content for your business
- Generating IVR and customer service audio
- Producing marketing and advertising content (with proper consent)
- Building products that integrate our API (per API terms)

### 5.2 Restricted
- Reselling generated content as a competing TTS service
- Bulk generation for spam or abuse
- Creating voice models for redistribution
